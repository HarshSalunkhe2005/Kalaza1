Founder's Message

Kalaza Care embodies a heartfelt mission to revolutionize elderly and post-hospital care in India. Stemming from personal experiences of witnessing the struggles of aging parents from afar, we recognized a critical gap in support systems for families like ours. Our journey began with a vision to alleviate the burdens faced by individuals balancing careers abroad with the responsibility of caring for their loved ones back home.

Kalaza Care is not just about providing care; it is about delivering dependable 24/7 assisted living and medical recovery support in a setting that feels like home. We are committed to upholding dignity, ensuring comfort and offering the right clinical care at every stage—while giving families the reassurance they need, even from a distance.

We invite you to be a part of our journey as we redefine care by seamlessly blending recovery, support, and everyday living into one compassionate experience.

Pravinkumar R. Patil,
BS, MA, MS, MBA, PharmD.
